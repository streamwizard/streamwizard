The clips page is where your highlights stop being a wall of thumbnails. Filter
them down, sort them how you think, and drop them into folders. This is the part
of StreamWizard you'll actually live in.

  <img src="/images/clips/clips-overview.png" alt="Clips page overview" />
</Frame>

## Grid or details

Top right of the page, a toggle switches between two layouts:

- **Grid:** thumbnail cards. Good for skimming visually.
- **Details:** a denser, row-based list. Good when you're scanning titles,
  views, and dates.

Your choice rides along in the URL, so a bookmarked or shared link keeps the
same view.

## Pulling in new clips

The **Sync** button next to the filters fetches your latest clips from Twitch on
demand. Handy if you didn't turn on auto-sync during
[onboarding](/onboarding), or you just finished a stream and don't want to wait.
